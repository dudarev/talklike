Talk Like ...

Here you will find subtitled videos from YouTube. Use them to improve your listening comprehension of foreign languages. With automatic translation service from Google, translate these subtitles to many other languages or look up unfamiliar words.

Presently, Talk Like Obama and Talk Like Putin pages are here.

Would you like to contribute more subtitles to help others to learn your native language? Give us feedback by email artem@talklike.com and stay tuned with twitter @talklike
